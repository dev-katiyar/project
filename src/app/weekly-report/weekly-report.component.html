US Market Sectors - Relative Analysis

This analysis uses the price ratio of sector ticker versus SPY to establish a relative technical score. 13 technical measures are applied to the ratio to determine the score. The score tells us how sector compares to another overall market. We used sector ETFs ticker against S&P 500 (SPY) to help assess relative strength or weakness of the sector.

Quite often scores of + 0.75 or greater are good times to reduce holdings and -0.75 or lower are good times to buy. However, scores can stay extremely overbought or extremely sold for a few weeks, so at times patience is required.

To dig deeper into each sector and for relative analysis of its contituent tickers, please follow the link: Relative Analysis.

Overbought - could be a good time to reduce holdings. Oversold - could be a good time to increase holdings
