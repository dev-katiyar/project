About Absolute Analysis

This analysis uses the price of the securities to establish an absolute technical score. 13 technical measures are applied to the price data to determine the score.

Quite often scores of + .75 or greater are good times to reduce holdings and -.75 are good times to buy. However, scores can stay extremely overbought or sold for a few weeks so at times patience is required.
