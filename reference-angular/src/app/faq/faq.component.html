1. How to Change my Password?

You can easily change your password by following below steps:

  • Click your name on the right hand corner.

  • Click on My Account.

  • On Account Details tab enter your new password and then click on Save button on right hand corner.

2. How to Change my Account information?

You can easily change your account information by following below steps:

  • Click your name on the right hand corner.

  • Click on My Account.

  • On Account Details tab edit your information and then click on Save button on right hand corner.

3. How to Change my Plan?

You can easily change your plan by following below steps:

  • Click your name on the right hand corner.

  • Click on My Account.

  • Click on Subscription tab.

  • Click on Change Plan button.

  • This will open a Upgrade Subscription Plan window. Select and Click on the Plan you want and then click on the Change button.

4. How to Unsubscribe my Plan?

You can easily unsubscribe from your plan by following below steps:

  • Click your name on the right hand corner.

  • Click on My Account.

  • Click on Subscription tab.

  • Click Unsubscribe button.

5. How to update Credit Card?

You can easily update your credit card information by following below steps:

  • Click your name on the right hand corner.

  • Click on My Account.

  • Click on Update Credit Card tab.

  • Fill all the information on the form and press Submit button.

6. How to Subscribe / Unsubscribe Daily Emails?

You can easily Subscribe/Unsubscribe from daily emails by following below steps:

  • Click your name on the right hand corner.

  • Click on My Account.

  • Click on Update Daily Emails tab.

  • Click on Subscribe Email or Unsubscribe Email.
