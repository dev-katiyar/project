Commonly Asked Questions
What’s The Cost?

The automated portfolio management service is 0.50% annually billed quarterly.

How Do I Change My Portfolio Model?

To change your model at any time, send a request to contact@simplevisor.com. The trading team will rebalance your current portfolio into the model you requested.

Do You Have Fractional Share Trading?

Yes. We offer fractional share trading so you can own the shares of higher-priced stocks in your portfolio model.

Can I Link My Other Accounts To My Account Portal?

Yes. Once you create your SimpleVisor.com trading account, you can link accounts held at TD Ameritrade, Schwab, and Fidelity to your portal for consolidated viewing.

Do You Have An App For My Phone?

Yes. We will soon provide an app to allow you access to your SimpleVisor.com accounts via your mobile device.

How Do I Roll Over A 401k Plan?

If you have a 401k plan you want to roll over follow these steps or send a request by clicking here

How Do I Transfer An Existing Account To SimpleVisor?

Once you register, go to your client portal and on the left-hand side click ACAT. Or, send a request by clicking here

Once My Account Is Opened Is It Immediately Put Into My Model Allocation?

No. We look for market pullbacks to onboard accounts into your selected model.

How Often Are Portfolio Models Rebalanced?

The SimpleVisor team rebalances all models each quarter.

I Need Help Deciding What’s Best For Me?
